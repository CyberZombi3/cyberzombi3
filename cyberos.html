> OVERVIEW

CyberOS is a custom 32‑bit operating system built from scratch. Multi‑stage bootloader, VBE graphics, RTL8139 networking, and a lightweight shell. Designed as a cyber‑lab OS for experiments, hacking tools, and low‑level development.
